Abeeway Smart Badge

By Abeeway

The Abeeway Smart Badge is a wearable LoRaWAN® multi-mode tracker with embedded sensors combining various location tracking technologies such as GPS, Low-power GPS, Wi-Fi Sniffer, BLE, and LoRaWAN TDoA. In addition to that, the Abeeway Smart Badge features a temperature sensor, SOS panic button, and a buzzer.
