RS485-LN

By Dragino Technology Co., Limited

The Dragino RS485-LN is an RS485 to LoRaWAN® converter. It allows the RS485 devices to use LoRaWAN wireless protocol which simplifies the IoT installation and reduces the installation/maintaining cost.
