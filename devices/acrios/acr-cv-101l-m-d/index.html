M-Bus IoT converter

By ACRIOS Systems s.r.o.

The Acrios M-Bus IoT converter is an M-Bus to LoRaWAN® converter that can be used for reading electricity meters and calorimeters. The M-Bus converter supports connections of up to 5 UL (Unit Loads) and communication is based on the EN 137 57-3 protocol.
