R718LB - Wireless Hall Type Open/Close

By Netvox Technology Co., Ltd

The Netvox R718LB - Hall Type Open/Close is a LoRaWAN® end device that consists of a hall-effect sensor to detect the open and close state of anything, for example detecting the opening and closing of doors and windows.
